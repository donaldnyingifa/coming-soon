ABOUT US

'Teez and Feelz' is a pro-modern lifestyle and urban streetwear company founded on the principles of liberalism and individual autonomy and we believe in a self-designed life as against an externally imposed one.

MISCHIEF is how we sell! TOLERANCE and FREE-WILL however is at our core!!

THE DREAM

Our dream is seeing young people truly acknowledging their true and innate identity which society so often tends to suppress in the name of traditions and culture, because we believe true collective growth is achieved when originality and diversity is embraced.

MISSION

'Teez and Feelz' aims to mobilize and connect with progressive and young liberals of all definition, gender, and status, through non-conformist and youth-friendly products with the hope that they recognize the importance of banding together to take a stand against cultural oppression that subdues our innate values, behaviours, and essence.
